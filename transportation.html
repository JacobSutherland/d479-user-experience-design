Transportation (Arriving In Taniti)

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Taniti Airport

Welcome to Taniti Airport, your gateway to the enchanting island paradise of Taniti. Nestled amidst the lush tropical landscapes and azure waters of the South Pacific, Taniti Airport is a charming and convenient hub for travelers arriving by small jets and propeller planes, providing easy access to all the wonders that our island has to offer. Despite its smaller size, Taniti Airport is equipped with modern amenities and facilities to ensure a seamless and comfortable travel experience for visitors from near and far. Upon arrival, travelers are greeted by the warm tropical breeze and stunning views of the surrounding landscape, setting the tone for a memorable stay in paradise.

Taniti Seaport

the turquoise waters of the South Pacific welcome visitors from around the globe to our island paradise. Situated along the picturesque coastline of Taniti, our bustling seaport serves as a vibrant gateway for travelers arriving by ship, providing a warm island welcome and easy access to all the wonders that our tropical paradise has to offer. As ships glide gracefully into the harbor, passengers are greeted by the sight of swaying palm trees, golden beaches, and lush greenery stretching as far as the eye can see. The air is filled with the sounds of laughter and excitement as visitors disembark, eager to explore the beauty and culture of Taniti. Taniti Seaport is equipped with modern facilities and services to ensure a smooth and seamless arrival experience for passengers. From spacious docking facilities capable of accommodating cruise ships of all sizes to efficient customs and immigration procedures, our seaport is committed to providing a welcoming and hassle-free experience for travelers from near and far.
