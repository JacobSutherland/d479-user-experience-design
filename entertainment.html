Entertainment

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are other things to do, including visiting a local history museum, going on chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, zip-lining in the rainforest, visiting several pubs, including a microbrewery, dancing at a new dance club, seeing a movie, taking helicopter rides, playing at an arcade, visiting art galleries, and bowling. Also, a nine-hole golf course should be operational by next year. Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Taniti History Museum

The Taniti Historical Museum is where the past comes to life in a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of our island's history. Nestled in the heart of the city, this cultural gem invites visitors to embark on an immersive exploration of Taniti's fascinating heritage, from ancient roots to modern-day marvels. Step through the museum's grand entrance and be transported back in time as you wander through meticulously curated exhibits that showcase the diverse cultures and civilizations that have shaped Taniti throughout the ages. Trace the footsteps of our ancestors as you journey through the museum's galleries, where artifacts, photographs, and interactive displays offer glimpses into the island's storied past.

Fishing

Situated in the heart of the South Pacific, Taniti is a haven for anglers of all skill levels, offering a diverse array of fishing experiences that promise excitement, adventure, and unforgettable memories. Cast your line into the azure waters surrounding Taniti and prepare to reel in the catch of a lifetime. Whether you prefer deep-sea fishing, fly fishing, or shoreline casting, there's something for every angler to enjoy in our pristine waters. For those seeking big-game thrills, deep-sea fishing charters offer the opportunity to hook into marlin, tuna, mahi-mahi, and other trophy species that roam the open ocean. Set sail aboard a well-equipped fishing vessel and venture into the depths, where the thrill of the chase and the anticipation of a record-breaking catch await.

Snorkeling

crystal-clear waters beckon you to dive into a world of wonder beneath the surface. Renowned for its vibrant coral reefs, colorful marine life, and pristine underwater landscapes, Taniti is a snorkeler's paradise like no other. Slip into the warm embrace of the South Pacific and prepare to embark on an unforgettable snorkeling adventure. With its calm, clear waters and abundant marine biodiversity, Taniti offers some of the best snorkeling opportunities in the world, suitable for beginners and experienced enthusiasts alike.

Rainforest Zip-lining

The thrill of adventure awaits amidst the lush green canopy of our pristine rainforest. Get ready to soar through the treetops like a tropical bird on an exhilarating zip-lining experience that will take your breath away and leave you with memories to last a lifetime. Nestled within the heart of Taniti's rainforest, our zip-lining course offers an adrenaline-pumping journey through the verdant canopy, where towering trees and exotic flora create the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable adventure. Strap into your harness, take a deep breath, and prepare to take flight as you launch from platform to platform, suspended high above the forest floor. Feel the rush of adrenaline as you zip effortlessly through the air, gliding past ancient trees and cascading waterfalls, with panoramic views of the lush rainforest stretching out before you. As you navigate the course, keep your eyes peeled for glimpses of colorful birds, playful monkeys, and other wildlife that call the canopy home.

Nightlife (Clubs, Pubs, Breweries, etc.)

The vibrant nightlife scene of Taniti, where the rhythm of the islands comes alive after dark. From bustling pubs and lively clubs to artisanal breweries, our city offers an eclectic array of entertainment options guaranteed to keep the party going until the early hours of the morning. Start your evening at one of Taniti's lively pubs, where you can unwind with a refreshing drink and soak up the laid-back island vibe. Whether you prefer a cozy tavern nestled in the heart of the city or a beachfront bar with views of the shimmering ocean, you'll find a pub to suit your mood and style. Sip on handcrafted cocktails made with fresh tropical fruits, sample local craft beers brewed with exotic ingredients, and mingle with friendly locals and fellow travelers as you swap stories of your adventures in paradise.
